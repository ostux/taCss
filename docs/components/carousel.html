The carousel is a slideshow for cycling through a series of content.

Example

Carousels don’t automatically normalize slide dimensions. As such, you need to use style="--carousel-image-widht:width;--carousel-image-height:height;" inline style with the picture witdh and height without any measurement unit or just two number witch follow the picture aspect ratio like: style="--carousel-image-widht:16;--carousel-image-height:9;" for 16:9.
With captions

Add captions to your slides easily with the .img-overlay element within after any .carousel-item. They can be easily hidden on smaller viewports, as shown below, with optional display utilities.
